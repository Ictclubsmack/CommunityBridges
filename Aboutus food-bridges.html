Who We Are

Community Bridges is a digital platform built to empower and promote Non Government Organizations and the vital impact they hold in Uganda. Created by passionate secondary school students in Uganda who were driven by a shared desire to make a difference in their communities.

Our Origin

What began as a simple idea to tackle food waste and food insecurity quickly grew into something much bigger—a platform with the power to connect, inform, and inspire change across the country. Originally envisioned as a way to link surplus food from donors to communities in need, our journey revealed a deeper challenge: the limited visibility and support faced by non-government organizations (NGOs) doing vital work on the ground. In response, Community Bridges evolved into a broader solution—one that not only addresses food insecurity, but also amplifies the reach and impact of NGOs and aid providers throughout Uganda.

Our Mission

Today, Community Bridges helps broadcast the activities and success stories of NGOs, showcasing their contributions and promoting awareness of their role in society. Through features like geolocation mapping, users can easily discover foodbanks, aid organizations, and support services in their area. We also provide progress tracking tools that enable NGOs to monitor their work, share their impact with donors, and strengthen their ability to secure funding.

Our Goal

At its heart, Community Bridges is about creating meaningful connections—between organizations and communities, between need and support, and between young changemakers and the future they’re building. We believe that by bridging these gaps, we can inspire greater collaboration, resilience, and impact across Uganda.
